What Would You Like To Know About VR?

The concept of Virtual Reality is mind blowing allowing the user to view a different world. The head sets create a visual field of 114 degrees that is equivalent to the human FOV. The system running the game is providing two full rendered 1080p 60fps seamless gameplay. While this is a beautiful technological advancement it comes with a price tag. The original headsets were $600-800 for the setup not including a $1000+ gaming desktop.

Pros and Cons of VR

    Pros:

  • Unparalleled Experience
  • First Person View Gaming
  • Interactive Room Scale Worlds
  • Great Party Activity

    Cons:

  • Price Tag (System and Headset $1500+)
  • Play area requirements
  • Motion Sickness
  • One Size Fits Most
